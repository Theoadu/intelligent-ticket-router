Corporate VPN Usage Guide

The company requires VPN usage for accessing internal systems from remote networks.

Installation Steps:
1. Download the VPN client from the internal software portal.
2. Authenticate using Single Sign-On (SSO).
3. Connect to the “CORP-SECURE” server.

Troubleshooting:
- Ensure internet connectivity.
- If connection fails repeatedly, restart the VPN client.
- Open a ticket with error logs attached for escalation.
