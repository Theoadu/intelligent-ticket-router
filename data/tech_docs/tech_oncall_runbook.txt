On-Call Engineer Runbook

Severity Levels:
- SEV1: Major outage impacting customers. Respond within 5 minutes.
- SEV2: Partial degradation. Respond within 15 minutes.
- SEV3: Minor bug or annoyance. Respond within business hours.

Escalation Steps:
1. Acknowledge alert.
2. Review alert details and logs.
3. Mitigate immediate customer-facing impact.
4. Notify engineering leadership for SEV1/SEV2.
5. Document root cause analysis in the incident tracker.
