IT Support FAQ

Q: My laptop is running slow. What should I do?
A: Restart the laptop, ensure available disk space, and close memory-heavy applications. 
If the issue persists, submit a support ticket.

Q: How do I reset my company email password?
A: Use the Password Reset Portal. If locked out, contact IT Support for identity verification.

Q: What should I do if I suspect a phishing email?
A: Do not click on any links. Forward the email to security@company.com immediately.
