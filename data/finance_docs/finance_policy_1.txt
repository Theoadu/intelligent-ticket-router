Finance: Expense & Reimbursement Policy
- Receipts required for all expenses > $10.
- Company credit card: submit reconciliation monthly.
- Travel expense: airfare must be economy class; manager pre-approval required.
- Payroll changes: banking updates must be sent to payroll@company.com with verification document.
- Budget approvals: small expenses < $1,000 by manager; > $1,000 require finance approval.
Procedure:
1. Submit expenses via expense portal, attach receipts.
2. Manager approves or rejects within 5 working days.
3. Finance processes reimbursements on next payroll run.
