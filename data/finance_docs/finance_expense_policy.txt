Corporate Expense Policy

1. Meal Reimbursements
Up to $50/day for domestic travel and $95/day for international travel.

2. Transport
Rideshare receipts are required. Only economy class flights are reimbursable.

3. Expense Submission
Employees must submit expenses within 30 days after travel. 
Late submissions may be rejected.

4. Non-Reimbursable Items
Personal entertainment, alcohol, and family travel expenses.
