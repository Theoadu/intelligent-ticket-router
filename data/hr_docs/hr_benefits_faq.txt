HR Benefits – Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I enroll in the company’s health insurance plan?
A: Enrollment occurs during onboarding or during the annual benefits renewal period in November.

Q: Does the company offer retirement contributions?
A: Yes. The company matches employee contributions up to 5% of base salary.

Q: What is the process for requesting study leave?
A: Employees must submit a request to HR at least 30 days in advance with proof of course enrollment.

Q: Are dependents covered under the company health insurance?
A: Yes, but dependent coverage requires an additional employee-paid premium.
