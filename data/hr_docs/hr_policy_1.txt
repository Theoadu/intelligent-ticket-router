HR Policy: Leave and Absence
- Annual leave accrual: employees accrue 1.75 days per month.
- Paternity leave: 5 working days paid; proof required: birth certificate + paternity declaration.
- Adoption leave: same as paternity leave; see HR portal for forms.
- Unpaid leave: must be approved by manager and HR.
Procedure for filing leave:
1. Fill the leave application form on HR portal.
2. Attach required documentation.
3. Submit to immediate manager for approval.
4. HR completes processing within 3 business days.
