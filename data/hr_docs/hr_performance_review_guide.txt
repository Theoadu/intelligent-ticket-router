Performance Review Guide

1. Review Cycle
Performance reviews occur twice a year: April and October.

2. Competency Framework
Employees are evaluated on Collaboration, Technical Ability, Ownership, Communication, and Innovation.

3. Rating Scale
1 = Needs Improvement
2 = Developing
3 = Meets Expectations
4 = Exceeds Expectations
5 = Exceptional

4. Promotion Eligibility
Employees must maintain at least a “4” rating in two consecutive cycles and demonstrate increased responsibility.

5. Appeal Process
Employees may request a review meeting with HR and their manager within 14 days of receiving their rating.
