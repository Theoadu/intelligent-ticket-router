Time Off Policy (Detailed)

Employees are responsible for logging all time-off requests through the HR Portal.
Approval is mandatory before taking leave.
Unapproved absences may result in disciplinary action.

Emergency Leave:
Up to 3 days per year; eligibility depends on circumstances.

Unpaid Leave:
Employees may apply for unpaid leave only after exhausting all paid leave categories.

Carryover Rules:
Employees may carry over up to 5 unused leave days into the next calendar year.
