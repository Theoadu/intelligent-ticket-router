Company HR Policy Overview

1. Working Hours & Attendance
Employees are expected to work 40 hours per week. Core hours are 10:00â€“16:00, Monday to Friday. 
Flexible working arrangements can be approved by direct managers.

2. Leave Entitlements
Annual leave: 21 working days per year.
Sick leave: Up to 10 days per year without medical note; beyond this requires documentation.
Parental leave: Available based on national legislation and internal maternity/paternity guidelines.

3. Remote Work Guidelines
Employees may request remote work privileges after 6 months of employment. 
Remote workers must be available during core hours and maintain stable internet connectivity.

4. Payroll and Compensation
Salaries are paid on the last working day of each month.
Bonus payments follow the quarterly performance cycle.

5. Conflict Resolution
Employees should initially attempt direct resolution with colleagues. 
Unresolved issues may be escalated to the HR Business Partner.
